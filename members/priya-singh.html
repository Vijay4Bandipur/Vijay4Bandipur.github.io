About Priya Singh

As a Commerce student with a specialization in Financial Management, I bring both academic knowledge and practical experience in financial administration to the Treasurer position. My commitment to transparency and accountability has been demonstrated through my role as Finance Coordinator for multiple student organizations.

Over the past four years, I have successfully managed budgets exceeding ₹5 lakhs without a single discrepancy. My experience includes budget planning, expense tracking, financial reporting, and ensuring compliance with institutional financial policies. I believe that students have the right to know how their money is being used.

My approach to financial management is built on three pillars: transparency, efficiency, and student welfare. Every rupee should be accounted for and every expenditure should benefit the student community directly or indirectly.

Financial Management Plan

Budget Transparency

Complete transparency in budget allocation and expenditure with regular public reporting and accessible financial records.

  • Monthly financial statements
  • Public budget dashboards
  • Expenditure categorization
  • Annual financial reports

Cost Optimization

Maximizing value from every rupee spent while maintaining quality and ensuring effective resource utilization.

  • Vendor comparison processes
  • Bulk purchasing benefits
  • Cost-benefit analysis
  • Waste reduction initiatives

Revenue Generation

  • Sponsorship opportunities
  • Fundraising events
  • Alumni contribution programs
  • Partnership revenue

Implementing modern digital tools for efficient financial management and real-time tracking.

  • Online expense tracking
  • Digital payment systems
  • Automated reporting
  • Mobile finance apps

  • Secure banking practices
